✦ The Imprint

A house built
on lead and linen.

Ironwell & Vance was founded in London in 1894 by a retired naval officer and a Cambridge economist who could not agree on a single matter, save that the books of their respective fields were being printed too cheaply, and too quickly.

We print on Mohawk Superfine and Zerkall mould-made stocks. We bind in linen cloth and quarter-leather. We refuse manuscripts that cannot defend themselves before our editorial council. We have done so, without significant deviation, for one hundred and thirty years.

— The Editors, Bloomsbury & New Haven
